III. Journey Overview

Paul's First Missionary Journey Route

  1. Antioch (Syria) - Departure point (Acts 13:1-3)
  2. Seleucia - Port city, 16 miles from Antioch (Acts 13:4)
  3. Salamis (Cyprus) - "And when they were at Salamis, they preached the word of God in the synagogues of the Jews" (Acts 13:5)
  4. Paphos (Cyprus) - Encounter with Bar-Jesus (Elymas), conversion of Sergius Paulus (Acts 13:6-12)
  5. Perga (Pamphylia) - John Mark departs to Jerusalem (Acts 13:13)
  6. Antioch in Pisidia - First recorded sermon, mixed response (Acts 13:14-52)
