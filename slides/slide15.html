VI. Theological Significance

Expansion to Gentiles

First major effort to purposefully evangelize non-Jewish regions, fulfilling Jesus's commission: "But ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and ye shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth" (Acts 1:8)

Apostolic Authority

Paul proclaims the Gospel with boldness amidst persecution, authenticates apostleship with "signs and wonders, and mighty deeds" (2 Corinthians 12:12), fulfilling Jesus's promise that "these signs shall follow them that believe" (Mark 16:17)
