V. Key Participants

Name Role
Paul Lead missionary, preacher, theologian; described himself as "apostle of the Gentiles" (Romans 11:13)
Barnabas Paul's companion, encourager ("Son of Consolation," Acts 4:36), co-evangelist; Levite from Cyprus who sold land to support the early church (Acts 4:36-37)
John Mark Assistant/helper who departed early (Acts 13:13); sister's son to Barnabas (Colossians 4:10); later wrote the Gospel of Mark and became "profitable" to Paul (2 Timothy 4:11)
Sergius Paulus Roman proconsul of Cyprus and convert; described as "a prudent man" (Acts 13:7); archaeological evidence confirms his historical existence
