IV. Key Events: Antioch in Pisidia

Antioch in Pisidia

  • Paul delivers his first recorded sermon in Acts (13:16-41)
  • Structure: Old Testament history (13:17-25), Jesus as fulfillment (13:26-37), justification through Christ (13:38-41)
  • Quotes four Old Testament passages: Psalm 2:7, Isaiah 55:3, Psalm 16:10, and Habakkuk 1:5
  • Some Jews accept, others become jealous and stir persecution: "But when the Jews saw the multitudes, they were filled with envy" (Acts 13:45)
  • Paul and Barnabas boldly declare: "It was necessary that the word of God should first have been spoken to you: but seeing ye put it from you, and judge yourselves unworthy of everlasting life, lo, we turn to the Gentiles" (Acts 13:46)
  • Gentiles rejoice: "I have set thee to be a light of the Gentiles, that thou shouldest be for salvation unto the ends of the earth" (Isaiah 49:6, quoted in Acts 13:47)
