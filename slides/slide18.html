VIII. Timeline

~46 A.D.

Departure from Antioch

"As they ministered to the Lord, and fasted, the Holy Ghost said, Separate me Barnabas and Saul for the work whereunto I have called them" (Acts 13:2)

46-48 A.D.

Ministry in Cyprus and Asia Minor

Preaching, miracles, church planting, persecution; historical context: This occurred during the reign of Emperor Claudius (41-54 A.D.), a relatively stable period in the Roman Empire that facilitated travel and communication

~48 A.D.

Return to Antioch

"And when they were come, and had gathered the church together, they rehearsed all that God had done with them, and how he had opened the door of faith unto the Gentiles" (Acts 14:27); this report likely influenced the Jerusalem Council's decisions (Acts 15) regarding Gentile believers
