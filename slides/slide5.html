III. Journey Overview (Continued)

  1. Iconium - "A great multitude both of the Jews and also of the Greeks believed" (Acts 14:1)
  2. Lystra - Healing miracle, mistaken for gods, Paul stoned (Acts 14:6-20)
  3. Derbe - "And when they had preached the gospel to that city, and had taught many" (Acts 14:21)
  4. Return trip - Retraced steps, "ordained them elders in every church" (Acts 14:23)
  5. Attalia - Coastal city, set sail back (Acts 14:25-26)
  6. Antioch (Syria) - "And when they were come, and had gathered the church together, they rehearsed all that God had done with them" (Acts 14:27)
